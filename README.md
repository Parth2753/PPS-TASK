# 🚀 PPS TASK – Task Projects

## 📌 About
This repository contains Python-based mini projects developed as part of the **Programming for Problem-Solving Laboratory (ACSE07)**.

These projects focus on solving real-world problems using basic programming concepts like loops, conditions, and functions.

---

## 📂 Projects Included

### 💧 1. Daily Water Intake Tracker
A simple program to monitor daily water consumption and check hydration status.

**Features:**
- Tracks water intake
- Displays hydration level
- Simple console-based system

---

### 📥📤 Input & Output

**Input:**  
Enter water consumed today (in liters): 1.8  

**Output:**  
Hydration Status:  
Adequately Hydrated  
Good job! You are meeting your daily water requirement  

---

### 🛒 2. Smart Grocery Bill Estimator
Calculates total grocery bill including discount and tax.

**Features:**
- Multiple item input
- Discount calculation
- Tax addition
- Final bill summary

---

### 📥📤 Input & Output

**Input:**  
Enter number of items: 3  
Enter price of item 1: 120  
Enter price of item 2: 80  
Enter price of item 3: 50  

**Output:**  
Bill Summary:  
Total Amount: ₹250  
Discount Applied: ₹25  
Tax Added: ₹11.25  
Final Payable Amount: ₹236.25  

---

### 🌾 3. Crop Price Trend Analyzer
Analyzes crop prices over multiple days and suggests decisions.

**Features:**
- Detects trends (Increasing / Decreasing / Fluctuating)
- Suggests: SELL / WAIT / HOLD
- Helps in decision making

---

### 📥📤 Input & Output

**Input:**  
Enter number of days: 4  
Enter price for day 1: 20  
Enter price for day 2: 25  
Enter price for day 3: 30  
Enter price for day 4: 35  

**Output:**  
Market Analysis:  
Trend: Increasing  
Decision: SELL
